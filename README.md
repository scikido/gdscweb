# Google Developers Student Club - GH Raisoni College of Engineering & Management

This GitHub Organization represents GDSC-GHRCEM, an official community group at the university level dedicated to students who have an interest in Google developer technologies. We welcome students from various undergraduate and postgraduate programs who are keen on advancing their skills as developers. Being part of GDSC offers students the opportunity to expand their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and collaborate on developing solutions.
